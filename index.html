I am a Network Software Engineering Specialist at Telstra and a research collaborator with Dr. Sunny Rai (University of Pennsylvania). My research experience and interests include natural language processing, multilingual understanding, figurative language, reasoning, social media analysis, and AI agent development. In industry, I design and deploy production-grade backend and frontend systems, and have been recognized at Telstra for initiative, teamwork, and ownership.

I also build AI-powered applications such as NutriPal and AlarmPal, reflecting my interest in multimodal learning, AI agents, and human–AI interaction.

I enjoy tackling AI problems that require deep research insight and have the potential for real-world impact, and I continually explore new directions and collaborations that push my understanding forward.

Previously, I completed my Bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering at Mahindra University, where I was a research assistant under Dr. Sunny Rai.

Ongoing Projects

                
                

                    
AlarmPal

                    
Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Google Assistant, Gemini AI • 2025

                    

                        A smart Android alarm clock built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose, featuring integration with 
                        Google Gemini for voice-controlled alarm creation. It includes AI-powered wake-up 
                        assistance that delivers personalized weather or calendar updates via text-to-speech, along with 
                        smart challenges (math puzzles, QR scanning, shake detection, memory games) to ensure you actually 
                        get up. The app also includes a built-in Pomodoro timer and fully customizable themes for a 
                        personalized user experience.
                    

                    

                        Current Status: In Build

NutriPal

                    
React Native, Expo, FastAPI, Google Gemini Vision AI • 2025

                    

                        A modern AI-powered mobile nutrition tracking app that automatically analyzes food images or food names, and provides 
                        relatively accurate calorie and macronutrient breakdowns. The frontend is built with React Native and 
                        Expo for a seamless cross-platform experience, while the backend uses FastAPI and Google's Gemini Vision 
                        AI with multiple specialized agents to identify ingredients, calculate calories, and deliver personalized 
                        nutritional insights.
                    

                    

                        Current Status: Available on Android (Play Store Alpha track) and iOS (TestFlight) for private testing with a limited user group. Future public release planned following additional feature and performance updates.
